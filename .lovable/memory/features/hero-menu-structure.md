The homepage hero features exactly four primary entry points: Monthly Forecast (תחזית חודשית — free, name+gender+birthDate only), Compatibility (התאמה זוגית), Tarot (פתיחת טארוט), and Full Astrological Chart (מפה אסטרולוגית מלאה — premium, requires all 5 fields including birth time and place).

Monthly Forecast is a simplified free flow that does NOT calculate a natal chart or require birth time/place. It generates a simple personal monthly reading based on sun sign only.

Full Astrological Chart (BirthChartModal) is the premium path that calculates the full natal chart with all planetary positions, houses, and aspects. It requires name, gender, birth date, birth time, and birth place.

Palm Reading is no longer a hero entry point. Logic for these entry points is managed in 'HeroSection.tsx'.
